What Physics Actually Allows

Three distinct things get called "teleportation." They have very different physical statuses.

Established Quantum state teleportation — demonstrated at 1,400 km

Theoretical Morris-Thorne traversable wormholes — valid GR math, requires Jupiter-mass exotic matter

Theoretical ER = EPR conjecture — spacetime geometry may emerge from entanglement

Speculative Matter scan-transmit-reconstruct — conceptually coherent, physically intractable

Speculative Warp drive (Alcubierre metric) — valid GR math, exotic matter ~10⁶⁴ kg required

Key Numbers

  • Human body: ~7 × 10²⁷ atoms → ~10²⁸–10³² bits to describe quantum state
  • Rest-mass energy of 70 kg human: ~6.3 × 10¹⁸ J (= mc²)
  • Exotic matter for 1 m wormhole throat: ~−2 × 10²⁷ kg (≈ Jupiter mass)
  • Alcubierre warp bubble (original): ~−10⁶⁴ kg exotic matter required
  • Casimir effect density (1 μm plates): ~−1.3 × 10⁻³ J/m³ — 38 orders too small
  • Decoherence time (macroscopic body, room temp): ~10⁻²³ s
  • Quantum teleportation record distance: 1,400 km (Micius, 2017)
