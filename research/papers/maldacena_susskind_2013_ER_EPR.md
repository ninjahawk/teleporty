# Cool Horizons for Entangled Black Holes (ER = EPR)

**Authors:** Juan Maldacena, Leonard Susskind  
**Year:** 2013 (submitted June 3, 2013; revised July 11, 2013)  
**Published:** Fortschritte der Physik, 61(9), 781–811 (2013)  
**arXiv:** 1306.0533  
**DOI:** 10.1002/prop.201300020

---

## Abstract
Proposes that Einstein-Rosen bridges (wormholes, "ER") and Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen pairs (entangled particle pairs, "EPR") are the same physical phenomenon viewed through different lenses. The conjecture: **ER = EPR** — any two maximally entangled particles are connected by a (Planck-scale, non-traversable) wormhole. More broadly, the structure of spacetime itself is woven from quantum entanglement.

---

## The Core Conjecture

### Setup
Consider two black holes formed from a single system in a maximally entangled state — the "thermofield double" state:

```
|TFD⟩ = (1/√Z) Σ_n e^(-βE_n/2) |n⟩_L |n⟩_R
```

Where:
- `|n⟩_L`, `|n⟩_R` — energy eigenstates of left and right black holes
- `β` — inverse temperature
- `Z` — partition function

This state is the Hartle-Hawking vacuum of the eternal Schwarzschild-AdS black hole, which is geometrically an Einstein-Rosen bridge (a wormhole connecting two exterior regions).

### The ER = EPR Statement
Maldacena & Susskind argue that the entanglement in `|TFD⟩` **is** the geometric wormhole — they are the same thing. Extending this: any EPR pair of entangled particles corresponds to a (microscopic, non-traversable) ER bridge between them.

**Implication:** Spacetime connectivity = quantum entanglement. If you destroy entanglement between two regions, you disconnect them geometrically. Entanglement is the "glue" holding spacetime together.

## Wormhole Properties Implied by ER = EPR

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Scale | Planck scale (~10⁻³⁵ m) for particle-scale entanglement |
| Traversable? | **No** — these wormholes are non-traversable; nothing can pass through |
| Causal structure | Preserves causality; no superluminal signaling |
| Information transfer | Information cannot be sent via the ER bridge faster than light |
| Relation to large wormholes | Macroscopic traversable wormholes (Morris-Thorne type) are a separate, unrelated construction |

## Resolution of the AMPS Firewall Paradox

The ER=EPR conjecture was motivated in part by the AMPS (Almheiri-Marolf-Polchinski-Sully) firewall paradox (2012), which argued that Hawking radiation entanglement implies infalling observers hit a "firewall" of high-energy radiation at the horizon — contradicting GR's prediction of smooth horizon crossing.

Maldacena & Susskind propose that the ER bridge structure provides a way for the infalling observer's interior and the Hawking radiation's exterior to remain connected without creating a firewall, by treating the entanglement as a geometric connection rather than a local quantum-mechanical one.

## The 2022 "Holographic Wormhole" Experiment

**Paper:** Jafferis, Zlokapa et al. (2022), *Nature* 612, 51–55.  
"Traversable wormhole dynamics on a quantum processor"

Using 9 qubits on Google's Sycamore processor, the team implemented a version of the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev (SYK) model — a quantum system that has a holographic dual (an AdS₂ spacetime with a wormhole). They demonstrated that quantum teleportation between two SYK clusters could be interpreted as information passing through a wormhole in the holographic dual.

**What this is:**
- A simulation of wormhole *physics* in a toy holographic model
- The SYK model is a many-body quantum system, not a physical wormhole
- The "wormhole" exists only in the abstract holographic dual description
- Nothing physically traveled through a wormhole in spacetime

**Controversy:**
Kobrin, Schuster & Yao (2023), arXiv:2302.07897, critiqued the experiment, arguing:
- The learned Hamiltonian lacks proper thermalization properties expected from SYK
- The "size winding" signal is a generic feature of small commuting systems, not evidence of gravitational dynamics
- The gravitational interpretation is not uniquely supported by the data

Jafferis et al. responded (arXiv:2303.15423) that the critique is consistent with their main claims about size winding and scrambling.

## Implications for Teleportation

The ER=EPR conjecture, if correct, suggests a deep connection between quantum entanglement and spacetime geometry. For teleportation:

1. **Does not enable macroscopic teleportation.** The wormholes implied by ER=EPR are non-traversable and Planck-scale.
2. **Quantum teleportation via wormhole language.** The Bennett et al. quantum teleportation protocol can be re-described as information traversing a non-traversable wormhole — but this is a mathematical restatement, not a new capability.
3. **Future quantum gravity theories.** If ER=EPR is correct, a full quantum gravity theory might reveal new mechanisms for teleportation, but this is deeply speculative.

---

## Assessment

**Status:** Serious, actively researched theoretical physics conjecture. Published in a major journal, broadly cited, and has inspired significant work in quantum gravity and holography.

**What it proves:** Nothing yet — it is a conjecture, not a theorem.

**What it suggests:** Spacetime geometry may be emergent from quantum entanglement; ER bridges and EPR correlations may be the same thing.

**What it does NOT provide:** A mechanism for macroscopic matter teleportation, FTL signaling, or violation of causality.
