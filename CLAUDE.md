## Project Goal
Investigate the current state of teleportation science and attempt to design a physically realistic teleportation system grounded in actual physics — no handwaving, no sci-fi assumptions.

## Scope
- **Quantum teleportation**: entanglement-based state transfer (proven, operational)
- **Matter teleportation**: scanning, disassembly, transmission, and reconstruction of physical matter
- **Wormhole / spacetime approaches**: theoretical frameworks (Alcubierre, ER=EPR, traversable wormholes)
- **CIA / government declassified docs**: publicly released research (e.g., STARGATE, DIA/DIRD reports)
- **Original calculations**: energy budgets, information-theoretic limits, decoherence bounds

## Key Questions We're Investigating
1. What does physics actually allow — and what does it forbid?
2. What is the minimum energy/information cost to teleport a human-scale object?
3. Does quantum teleportation scale? What are the decoherence barriers?
4. What do declassified government documents actually claim, with what evidence?
5. Can we sketch a physically consistent architecture — even if far-future?

## Working Assumptions
- We follow the Standard Model + GR as our baseline. Extensions (string theory, etc.) are labeled speculative.
- No violations of conservation laws, no FTL without a proper framework.
- All calculations shown with units and sources.
